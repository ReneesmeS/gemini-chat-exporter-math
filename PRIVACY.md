# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Gemini Chat Exporter Math Chrome extension (the "Extension") handles data.

## Summary

- The Extension reads the content of the Gemini page you are viewing to generate an export file.
- The Extension does not transmit your conversation content to any external servers.
- The Extension does not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

## What the Extension Accesses

When you use the Extension on `https://gemini.google.com/`, it may access:

- The visible conversation content in the current Gemini page (text, formatting, code blocks, and math content) in order to export it.

## What the Extension Collects

The Extension does not collect personal information to a backend service.

- No account creation.
- No analytics collection.
- No tracking across websites.

## How Data Is Used

- Your data is processed locally in your browser to generate export files (for example, Markdown or Word).
- Exported files are saved using Chrome's download mechanism on your device.

## Data Storage and Retention

- The Extension does not store your conversation content on external servers.
- Any exported files are stored only where you save them on your own device.

## Data Sharing

The Extension does not share your data with third parties.

## Permissions

The Extension requests permissions required to:

- Run on Gemini (`https://gemini.google.com/`).
- Read the current page content to build an export.
- Trigger downloads to save the exported file.

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not send your conversation content to third-party services.

If you open links in documentation (for example, GitHub), those are handled by your browser and are governed by the privacy policies of those websites.

## Security

The Extension is designed to minimize data exposure by keeping processing on-device.

## Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If the Extension's data practices change, this Privacy Policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date will be revised.

## Contact

For questions or concerns about privacy, please open an issue in the project repository:
